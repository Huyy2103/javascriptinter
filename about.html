Meet the Fighters

Top UFC Fighters

Here are some of the most iconic and legendary fighters in UFC history.

Khabib Nurmagomedov

Khabib Nurmagomedov is an undefeated Russian fighter, former UFC Lightweight Champion, and one of the most dominant fighters in MMA history. He retired with an undefeated record of 29-0.

Islam Makhachev

Islam Makhachev is a Russian fighter and the current UFC Lightweight Champion. Known for his exceptional grappling and dominant wrestling, Makhachev is considered one of the best fighters in the lightweight division.

Khamzat Chimaev

Khamzat Chimaev is a rising star in the UFC, known for his ferocious fighting style and quick finishes. He competes in both the welterweight and middleweight divisions and has quickly become a fan favorite due to his aggressive approach to fighting.

Magomed Ankalaev

Magomed Ankalaev is a Russian fighter in the UFC's light heavyweight division. Known for his knockout power and well-rounded skill set, Ankalaev has become one of the top contenders in his weight class.
